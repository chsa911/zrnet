    My equipment

    There are some things that are necessary to make my reading dream true

    markers

    Once the cover is removed I still want to know which book I am reading. That's why I register my books

    in a google sheets and mark them with a barcode. I like to use markers with different colours to faster recognize them in my bookshelf.

    ducktape

    To secure loose pages i use a professional ducktape spender. It's invaluable to keep all pages in the

    right place.

    bookshelf

    My bookshelf is central part of my reading strategy. I can see my books with the markings that I applied and my books are dry and safe. If I want to read one I just take it out. If I have free space. I fill in new ones.

    reading bag

    When travelling I use a reading bag to store my books. It's better than a backpack as I can easily take the books out and also use it for trash.
