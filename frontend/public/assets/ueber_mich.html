What drives me to use a reading technique?

How the 3R technique helped me return to reading—more often, with better books.

As a kid, I devoured adventure stories—especially Karl May. Later, life got busy and reading slowly slipped away.

One day I discovered the 3R technique, and it brought books back into my daily routine. Suddenly even small breaks became reading time. And because I can move through books efficiently, my “bad habit” of coming home from bookshops and flea markets with a stack of books finally made sense.

What I love most: the 3R technique helps me find the truly good books faster. I can always keep a few options with me—so I’m never stuck without something worth reading.

Today I’m grateful for how many great books I’ve discovered this way—and I’m excited for the next ones.
