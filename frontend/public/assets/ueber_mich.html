What drives me to use such a reading technique?

    Already as a young boy I liked to read a lot favorably Karl May. Later on I lost this passion or rather time for it?

    One day I discovered the 3 R technique and I got into reading again as I could now use every minute to read. My vice to always return from bookshops and peddlars markets with too big heaps of books also stimulated this approach.

    This technique also helps me also to read only interesting books as I can carry always a lot of books with me to minimize the risk being without a good one.

    Today I am happy to have discovered a lot of interesting books thanks to this technique and looking forward to many more...
